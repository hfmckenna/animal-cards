Buzzard

While buzzards have congregated in groups of 15 or more this technically doesn't count as a flock. Flocks must consist of at least 20 birds.

  • Cry: peea-ay
  • Prey: Small mammals & carrion
  • Habitat: Moors, woodland & coast
  • Plumage: Muted

A common enough sight across much of Europe but nonetheless a welcome companion to a fell side hiker. While Buzzards lack the pomp and majesty of birds of prey like the eagle it's plaintive cry is nonetheless distinct.
